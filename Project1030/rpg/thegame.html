About

Welcome to Sweg Game. Here you can find any information about everything.
Step 1: Login or Create an Account

Step 2: Hit Play!

Use the Navigation Tab to navigate from the Home Screen to your Profile, Messages, and Settings

The Home Tab is used for the main screen and spawning a random item.

The Profile Tab is used to Create, Select, View, Edit and Delete Characters

The Setting Tab is where you can find information and set any settings within the game.

Notes:
Message functionality is not currently supported.

Known Bugs:
Takes two clicks to activate a button related function, not one.
